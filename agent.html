What is a cloud agent? How does it help enterprises move to the cloud?

The operating model of cloud agents is to use agency distribution, obtain authorization from the original manufacturer, and provide products and services to customers on behalf of the original manufacturer. The original factory and the cloud agent have a cooperative and mutually beneficial relationship. Original manufacturers can expand their market share through cloud agents, allowing them to serve as communication bridges with customers. Cloud agents can also make profits through commissions and price differences, gaining opportunities and competitive advantages in the local market.


However, if you can purchase directly from the original manufacturer, what is the difference between connecting with a cloud agent? Below, Hiyun has compiled the four major advantages for you!

Four major advantages of cloud agents

Cloud agents have four main advantages over direct and original factory purchases, including technical support, professional hosting services, one-stop service and preferential prices. The above advantages will be explained in detail below.


1.Technical support

The original manufacturer will provide cloud agents with training and support to ensure that they can fully understand and promote the product. Therefore, cloud agents have rich knowledge and experience and can provide customers with professional technology and services. For some enterprises, especially small and medium-sized enterprises, cloud agents can more effectively assist in cloud use and management without comprehensive resources and experience .


2.Professional hosting service

Cloud agents can help assess an enterprise's needs and environment, design cloud solutions appropriately, and assist with deployment and configuration. They will also assume responsibility for monitoring and managing systems to ensure security and compliance.


3.One-stop service

Enterprises can enjoy one-stop technical assistance provided by cloud agents from initial solution selection to equipment deployment and after-sales support, greatly simplifying the operation process and saving valuable time and manpower.


4.Discounted and transparent prices

When enterprises choose cloud services, cost is a necessary consideration. In terms of accounting review, cloud agents will provide invoices and transparent bills, allowing enterprises to clearly understand usage and expenditure details. In addition, since the original card payment is based on the international exchange rate, the price often fluctuates, while the cloud agent will pay a fixed price in Taiwan dollars and will also provide more diverse payment methods.

Cloud agency costs and solutions

Because cloud providers have large service volumes, the original manufacturers will provide them with more favorable prices. Therefore, customers can not only get more cost-effective pricing than the market, but also enjoy each provider's exclusive plans and discounts.
